About
The India Panchang skill on Alexa will now save you from the hassle of keeping a panchang handy and find each day to see how it is going to look like. At your fingertips, you can now find all the relevant information that you need about tithi from a panchang. The skill will tell you about tithis, whether it is a good time or subh muhurat to do something new. Along with muhurats, India Pachnang skill can also tell you about the inauspicious times of a day. This will help you to know which time to avoid if you wish to start or invest in something prosperous.
